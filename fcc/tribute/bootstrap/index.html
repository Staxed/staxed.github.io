Sheldon Cooper

Modern Day Genius

"I'm not insane, my mother had me tested." - Shelly

Things to know about Shelly...

  • He was born and raised in Galveston, Texas.
  • His mother is an overtly devout Evangelical Christian and his father is an alcoholic who was never around.
  • Sheldon was bullied by classmates and neighbors as a child, in part due to his status as a child prodigy...his behavioral quirks and lack of humility may have also played a role.
  • He is extremely fond of comic books...and costumes, roleplaying games, video games, card games, games, games, and games, action figures, fantasy, and most importantly Star Trek.
  • Has difficulty with friendship and emotions due to his massive intelligence and the resulting lack of social skills.
  • A Theoretical Physicist working mainly with String Theory.
