On top of the challenges detailed above, I will also be working towards completion of my Front End Developer Certificate through Free Code Camp. For those who have never heard of it, Free Code Camp is a community that was formed with the sole focus of helping people to learn to code, and then hopefully get experience by contributing to open source projects used by nonprofits.

Unlike some of the other free resources out there that are just simple tutorials, Free Code Camp is an indepth training environment that both teachs and forces you to go through exercises and multiple projects...learn by doing. If you want more information, checkout their website.
